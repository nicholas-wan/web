Day 0 - Departure

[28th June] We arrived at Perth International Airport after a 5h flight from Singapore . Picked up the Transperth SmartRider card (similar to EZ-Link card) for easy travel and headed to Fremantle. We needed to change lines to the Fremantle line and were confused on which stop to alight at. Eventually, we realized the trains from different lines used the same platform but were staggered.

About an hour later, we arrived at Fremantle and noticed that majority of stores were closed by 5.30pm . Our friendly hotel reception @ Quest Fremantle recommended some nice Western food at Benny's Bar and Cafe - Surf and Turf !

Day 1 - Fremantle

[29 June] Full Day to explore Fremantle. We stayed near the coast and it was easy to get around to our places of interest. We visited Bather's Bay first and saw many of Xian's worst enemies - birds! As Fremantle market was not opened on Thursday, we went to explore the other tourist attractions at Fremantle - WA Maritime Museum and Fremantle Prison .

As tourists, we also went to visit some instagram-worthy spots - Tourist's Wheel and The Containbow .
