Why Healthcare?
I attended a talk by the CEO of IHIS (Integrated Health Information Systems), Bruce Liang. The most impactful thing of the talk was that a day of work in the healthcare sector is a day of work to better Someone else's life.

In Singapore, there is a Patient to Doctor ratio of about 400 : 1, meaning that advancements in Healthcare Analytics are integral to increase efficiency and the allocation of Singapore's resources. Hence, a career in Healthcare Analytics is definitely a meaningful one that I would like to undertake.
