Day 2 - France

Paris

[24th October] Visited a very crowded Eiffel Tower in the morning, and had a good Chinese Zi-Char style lunch. Stopped by the Louvre Museum for some photos, then headed to Galeries Lafayette for some shopping and Cafe Food & Ice Cream, with a nice top floor view at The Terrace . Dinner was quite underwhelming with a bland tasting Steak, and I tried Escargots for the first time. Not a fan. Ended the day a night Seine River Cruise , but would have preferred to go for the cruise in the day as everything looked similar at night.

Day 4 - Netherlands

Zaandam | Amsterdam

[26th October] One of my favorite days of the Trip. Started off the day heading to the Windmill Village , where we saw a few demonstrations such as the Wooden Shoe Carving and the Cheese Factory . Later in the afternoon, we headed to a Fishing Village at Volendam for lunch. Another underwhelming meal with an overcooked steak :( Thereafter, we headed to Amsterdam for another River Cruise . At this point River Cruises were kind of boring so I switched to Japanese Audio for the Audio Tour to practice my Japanese. We also headed to Red Light District but no photos were allowed.

Day 6 - Germany Switzerland

Heidelberg | Schaffhausen

[28th October] Started off with a very cool day and visited Heidelberg Castle . Later, we drove past Germany's Black Forest and had some German pork knuckle for lunch. Also stopped by Lake Titisee for some photos, and we learned about the history of Cuckoo Clocks . We then drove to Switzerland's Rhine Falls and had some really delicious Chicken Tikka Masala that cost 18 Swiss Francs. So happy that I ate it because there was a traffic jam due to an accident and the bus driver also made a wrong turn which caused us to reach our hotel almost 3 hours later at around 9pm.

Day 7 - Switzerland

Engelberg | Lucerne

[29th October] One of the best days of the trip. Headed to an excursion at Mount Titlis where we took a cable car to the midway point then took the world's first Revolving Cable Car . There were quite a few activities at the mountain such as the Glacier Cave , Cliff Walk and the Ski Lift . Had a great lunch at the Panorama Restaurant, then we headed to Lucerne for some sightseeing. Ended the day with one of the most value for money meals - Pizza Poulet Curry which cost 13 Swiss Francs that the two of us shared and struggled to finish.
