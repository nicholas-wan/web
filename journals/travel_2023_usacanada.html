Day 1 - Arrival in New York

Route
Singapore Changi AirportSingapore→SQ24Singapore to New York→John F. Kennedy International AirportQueens→New York SubwayNew York City→Jersey CityNew Jersey

We reached John F. Kennedy International Airport after a 19-hour flight from Singapore. Thankfully, Singapore Airlines was a very comfortable ride with good food and Häagen-Dazs ice cream! The Uber to Clare's house cost 150 USD so we decided to take the subway. Along the way, we got lost a few times and took the train the wrong way. It was really confusing. After almost 4 hours, we finally arrived. We ordered Uber Eats from Fortune restaurant and had a quick meal.
